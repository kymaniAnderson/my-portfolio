About Me

Hello my friends on the internet! I am a full-stack web developer with a passion for developing telemedicine applications. I started my journey in web development like most people, with my very first "Hello World" program! Well, technically yes, but my story starts a little bit before that. In the summer of 2016, I decided to try out a hackathon that was being held with the belief that I would be making simple C code that would be run locally from the terminal.
To my surprise, there was way more to development than I had initially given credit for. Since then I've been attending hackathons and entrepreneurship competitions regularly, each further developing my understanding of problems and further motivating me to shift my educational focus to a field that would tackle real-world problems. I've completed my degree in Biomedical Engineering at the UWI and now I’m bringing my creative drive for solving problems with out-of-the-box solutions to the world, one project at a time!
